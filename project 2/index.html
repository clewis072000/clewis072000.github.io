About Us

Spin Class on Wheels serivce the community by bringing a high energy workout to your workplaces, party and orginzational events.
We combine cardiovascular exercise with the motivation of a group setting.

During the class participants ride stationary bikes in a group environment, led by a certified instructor.
The classes are designed to simulate outdoor cycling, incorporating various speeds, resistance levels, and routines to challenge riders of all fitness levels.

Benefits of workplace exercise

Increased Productivity

  • Better Focus and Concentration: Physical activity boosts blood flow to the brain, enhancing cognitive function and focus
  • Higher Energy Levels: Exercise can reduce fatigue and increase energy, leading to improved work performance

Mental health rewards

  • Stress Reduction: Physical activity releases endorphins, which can alleviate stress and improve mood.
  • Reduced Anxiety and Depression: Regular exercise can help reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression, leading to a more positive work environment.

Team Building

  • Strengthened Relationships: Group exercises or wellness challenges can foster teamwork and improve relationships among colleagues.
  • Improved Communication: Shared fitness goals can enhance communication and collaboration within teams.

Structure of a Class

  • Warm-Up: Classes usually begin with a brief warm-up to prepare the body for exercise, focusing on low resistance and a steady cadence.
  • Intervals and Drills: The instructor guides the class through a series of intervals, including:
    • Flat Roads: Fast, low-resistance cycling to build speed
    • Hill Climbs: Increased resistance to simulate uphill cycling, promoting strength and endurance.
    • Sprints: Short bursts of high-intensity pedaling to boost cardiovascular fitness.
  • Cool Down: The class concludes with a cool-down period, allowing participants to gradually lower their heart rate, often accompanied by stretching.
