JIM

Certifications: Certified Spinning Instructor, Personal Trainer

About Jim: With over 8 years in the fitness industry, Jim is dedicated to helping his riders reach their fitness goals.
He discovered spinning while searching for a way to stay fit during his college years, and he hasn’t looked back since!

Teaching Style: Jim brings an electrifying energy to his classes. His motivating approach and engaging playlists
creates an atmosphere that pushes participants to challenge themselves while having fun. He emphasizes form and technique
ensuring that everyone gets the most out of their workout.

Teaching Style: James brings an electrifying energy to his classes. His motivating approach and engaging playlists create an atmosphere

PAUL

Certifications: Certified Spinning Instructor, NASM Certified Personal Trainer

About Paul: Paul has been in the fitness industry for over 5 years, with a strong belief that fitness should be both challenging and enjoyable.
He discovered spinning as a way to combine his love for music and fitness, and he hasn’t looked back since!

Teaching Style: Known for his motivating presence, Paul creates a welcoming atmosphere in his classes. He focuses on pushing limits while
ensuring every rider feels supported. His carefully curated playlists keep the energy high and the workouts engaging!



TYRONE

Certifications: Certified Spinning Instructor, Group Fitness Instructor

About Tyrone: Tyrone has been passionate about fitness for over 6 years, finding his niche in spinning after trying it out at a local gym.
His enthusiasm for helping others achieve their fitness goals is what drives him every day.

Teaching Style: Tyrone classes are all about motivation and community. He combines challenging workouts with a friendly atmosphere encouraging
everyone to push themselves while celebrating progress together. His upbeat playlists feature everything from pop to hip-hop, ensuring a fun ride.
