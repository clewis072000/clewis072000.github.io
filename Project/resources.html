University Resources

  • Financial Literacy

    The following resources will help you understand your finances and develop your financial literacy. All students are encouraged to complete at least two modules of the “Money Management” program. Students should also view their loan indebtedness on NSLDS and use the Repayment Estimator to calculate the repayment amount as borrowing is continued. Students with questions should contact the Office of Student Financial Aid.

  • Scholarships

    The Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships at North Carolina Agricultural & Technical State University is a resource for current and prospective students seeking financial assistance. We pride ourselves in serving our students and the community. It is our desire for students to discover outside opportunities to assist in achieving their educational goals and objectives.

  • CST Scholarships

    The CST Departmental Scholarships are for new and returning students based on their academic achievement and financial need.
